Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 28 September 2023

This is the privacy policy for "Next - Logic Puzzles," a game developed by Lepton Apps. We create mobile apps, and "Next - Logic Puzzles" is our first app developed for the Android platform. At Lepton Apps, we take user privacy seriously and are committed to protecting the personal information of our users. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect the data collected through the use of "Next - Logic Puzzles." Please read this policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your personal information and how we will treat it. By using this game, you consent to our collection and use of your personal information as described in this policy.

Information we collect

This game collects information through the use of Google Mobile Ads (AdMob) SDK, an advertising platform owned by Google that allows us to display advertisements within the game. AdMob collects the following information automatically:

+ IP address: device's IP address.
+ User game interactions: including game launch, taps, and video views.
+ Diagnostic information: related to the performance of the game, including crash logs, game launch time, hang rate, and energy usage.
+ Device and Account identifiers: Android advertising (ad) ID, app set ID, and, if applicable, other identifiers
related to signed-in accounts on the device.

Please refer to the "Payments privacy and policy" section for further information on the use of the IP address.

How we use your information

The information collected through the use of AdMob is used for advertising, analytics, and fraud prevention purposes. AdMob uses this information to serve relevant ads, measure ad performance, and detect and prevent fraudulent activity.

+ The IP address may be used to estimate the general location of a device, such as the country or city.
+ User interactions with the game may be used to understand how users interact with ads and to serve more relevant ads based on user behavior.
+ Diagnostic information may be used to identify and troubleshoot technical issues with the game, such as crashes or slow performance.
+ Device and Account identifiers, such as the ad ID, may be used to deliver more relevant ads and to help prevent fraud.

For more information on how AdMob uses personal information, please visit:
+ https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites
+ https://policies.google.com/privacy#whycollect

Sharing of information

We do not share any personal information other than what is shared through AdMob. AdMob may share aggregated, non-personally identifiable information with third parties for advertising and analytics purposes.
By utilizing AdMob, we may share personal information with third-party service providers that perform services on behalf of AdMob, such as hosting or data analysis. However, these service providers are bound by confidentiality agreements and are not permitted to use your information for any other purposes.

You can refer to the following link for more details:
+ https://policies.google.com/privacy#infosharing

Children

Our game is not intended for children. It is designed for individuals aged 16 and over. Therefore, the advertisements in the game are also appropriate for this age group.

Security of user data

All user data collected by our game through the use of Google Mobile Ads (AdMob) SDK is transmitted securely using the Transport Layer Security (TLS) protocol, which encrypts data in transit to prevent unauthorized access.

Data retention and deletion

Our game uses AdMob, which is a service provided by Google. AdMob's policy states that they retain user data for as long as necessary to provide their services and delete user data under their guidelines and applicable law.

Google's data retention policy:
+ https://policies.google.com/privacy#inforetaining

Google's data deletion policy:
+ https://policies.google.com/privacy#infodelete

For more information, see the next section about advertisements.

Advertisements

+ Our game is free and supported by advertisements. We may display ads within the game, including personalized ads based on your interest and behavior. You can opt out of personalized advertising by following these steps:

- Go to your device settings.
- Go to "Google" settings.
- In the "Ads" section, choose "Reset advertising ID" or "Delete advertising ID".

Please note that opting out of personalized ads does not mean you will no longer receive ads, but they may be less relevant to your interests.

+ If you prefer not to see any ads in the game, you can purchase our "No Ads" product. For more information, please refer to the "Payments privacy and policy" section.

+ EEA and UK users: If you are a user in the European Economic Area (EEA) or the United Kingdom (UK), you have the right to control your ad preferences. In the game's main menu, go to "Settings" and tap on the "Ads Settings" option to change your preferences.

For more information on Google advertisements, visit this page:
+ https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

Payments privacy and policy

+ Our game offers a "No Ads" in-app product that provides an ad-free experience and gives you access to all hints in the game. We use the Google Play billing system to process payments for this product. The only information we receive from the billing system is whether you have purchased the product or not.

+ Our game is designed to be short, and users can complete it in less than three hours. As a result, we do not offer refunds for purchases of the "No Ads" product.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

Any updates or changes to the privacy policy for "Next - Logic Puzzles" will be posted on this page. We encourage you to check this page periodically to stay informed of any updates.

Contact us

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please don't hesitate to contact us. You can reach us by email at support@leptonapps.com. We will do our best to address any concerns you may have.
