Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 4 August 2024

This is the privacy policy for "Next - Brain Teasers," a paid puzzle game developed by Lepton Apps. This policy outlines our commitment to protecting the privacy of our players.
By downloading and using "Next - Brain Teasers," you agree to the terms described in this privacy policy. Please read this document carefully to understand our practices regarding the handling of any user information.

No User Data Collection or Sharing

"Next - Brain Teasers" does not collect or share any personal information from its players. We do not require users to provide any data, nor do we track, store, or share any information about the individuals who download and play our game.

Children

Our game is not intended for children. Our game is designed for individuals aged 16 and over. However, the content of the game does not contain any material that would be unsuitable for children.

Deletion of Data

As stated above, our game does not store any personal or device data. However, if you wish to delete your game data and reset your progress as if you had just installed the game, please follow these steps:

1- Start the game (go to the main menu).
2- Select "Settings".
3- Choose "Reset Game".
4- Select "YES".

Please note that resetting the game will result in the loss of all your progress.

Refund Policy

"Next - Brain Teasers" is a paid, short puzzle game that does not contain any in-app items or advertisements. Given the nature of our game, we do not offer refunds for purchases of our game beyond the default refund policy provided by Google Play.
If you are not fully satisfied with your purchase of "Next", you can request a refund through the Google Play Store according to their standard refund process.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

Any updates or changes to the privacy policy for "Next - Brain Teasers" will be posted on this page. We encourage you to check this page periodically to stay informed of any updates.

Contact us

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please don't hesitate to contact us. You can reach us by email at support@leptonapps.com. We will do our best to address any concerns you may have.
